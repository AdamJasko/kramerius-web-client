The Digital Library of the Czech Academy of Sciences

contains selected periodicals and monographs from the collection of the Library of the Czech Academy of Sciences in electronic form. It also contains books and magazines produced by departments of the Czech Academy of Sciences and current editions of selected academic periodicals.

The viewing of documents is limited by access rights. Information on documents (key words, abstracts, etc.) is available to all, as are complete documents that are not protected by copyright. Documents protected by copyright may be accessed in full only on computers in the study of the Library of the Czech Academy of Sciences.
